ABOUT WBC
World Business Conclave is a renowned name in culminating Intellectual Property Awards and Conclaves on both national and global platforms. All the events organized are aimed towards creating a space for enhanced brand communication and positioning using industry-leading technology, customer support and blue-chip networking. We build a space where independent creative professionals can support each other and grow together.

These award functions and conclaves we organize cover the agenda of concurrent issues, discuss the concerns, and showcase them through different media channels across the world. Thought-provoking panel discussions by influential personalities and dignitaries across key business industries were a distinct feature of the Conclave.

Why Choose Social C-Suite?

With vivid illustration, it will explore the most creative innovations of popular business of 2023.
