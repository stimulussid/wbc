Social C-Suite Convention and Awards

After two successful IP’s, two Awards and two Conclaves, Stimulus Research Services in media partnership with ET Now proudly presents Asia Pacific Convention & Awards 2023-24.

When: 14th Feb, 2024

Location: Pullman Aerocity, Delhi

These awards aim to recognize and reward the contribution made by CXOs, MD’s and other esteemed industry professionals who have played an essential role in the exponential growth of different industries.


The felicitation and encouragement is for those who have shown courage and willingness to take risks and solved a number of underlying problems with strategic decisions in their specific sectors to create a niche for themselves.

What we have to offer

  1. World Business Conclave Chapter 3
  2. Coffee Table Book: Creating 100 epic stories for the CXO’s who are the powerful leaders of tomorrow.

Stimulus Research Services is proud to announce the launch of the most coveted insignia as the compilation of the best 100 CXO’s from different sectors from Asia Pacific. This year will be a research driven power book by the editorial and research team of Stimulus and the selected jury from all the sectors.

This CXO Bible will help a lot of other sectors to learn from their inspirational stories and experiences. They will be showcased as thought leaders and veterans from their respective industries.

With vivid illustration, it will explore the most creative innovations of popular business of 2023.
