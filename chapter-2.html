Panel Discussion

The GLOBE Platinum Awards 2016 is an Asia-Pacific initiative that acknowledges and rewards brands and leaders across the Asia-Pacific region for their business excellence in taking their companies through the path of success with innovative approach and strong work ethics. PricewaterhouseCoopers P.L. (PwC), which is a multinational integrated professional services organisation and one of the biggest international auditing firms, will be the process reviewer for the complete project.

It will not only be reviewing the brands for their innovation and achievements but will also be evaluating the comprehensive excellence of the businesses in their respective domains. The GLOBE Platinum Awards 2016 will encompass the whole gamut of businesses across the Asia-Pacific region, with special focus on the service and infrastructure industries.
