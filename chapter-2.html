Global Luxuries Decode

Symbolism plays a significant role when it comes to lending a face to ideas and perceived themes in the mind. It not only adds a subtle dimension to concept building but also stamps the concept in the mind of the audience. With the same intent, the logo of the GLOBE LUXURIÉ DÉCODÉ 2017 has been conceived. It aptly construes the philosophy behind this two-day luxury summit.

The skilful use of geometrical shapes, stressing luxury and aesthetics depicted by diamonds; the bold use of colours black and gold, emphasising the richness and grandeur associated with the event; the intentional use of archaic French with accents, reinstating the beauty of classic; and positioning of a flower amidst linearity, putting emphasis on the budding aspirations for luxury aptly highlight the vision behind the organisation of this summit. And the phrase 'A FANFARE AFFAIR' modestly states the brand promise this luxury summit holds.

Why Dubai

Complementing the grandeur of the city, which itself is no less than an oasis of luxury in the Middle East, the GLOBE LUXURIÉ DÉCODÉ 2017 will bring in all flavours of luxury to satiate the hunger for luxury in the most aspiring set of audience. The city, which speaks with pride of its man-made islands, Palm Islands, tallest tower in the world, Burj Khalifa, and unmatched desire for luxurious lifestyle has today become the most sought-after travel destination for luxury lovers. One of the most futuristic cities on the world map, Dubai can aptly be named the luxury capital of the world.

The city, since decades, has been driving the luxury market of the Middle East with balanced contribution from Abu Dhabi. In a recent report published by Deloitte, it has been aptly highlighted that besides the rapid expansion of the luxury markets in India and Mexico, the premium luxury goods market of the Middle East is indicating a future growth potential. This, in a way, is giving a positive signal that premium brands from across the globe can now find their home sweet home in the markets of the Middle East, which essentially mean Abu Dhabi and Dubai. Nicknamed as the 'City of Gold,' Dubai aptly makes for the venue for this luxury summit.

ABOUT GLOBE LUXURIE CONCLAVE 2017

Celebrating the opulence, splendour and extravagance of the most sough-after luxury experiences in India and UAE, the GLOBE LUXURIÉ DÉCODÉ 2017 will decode the latest luxury trends, drifts and orientation in the ever-changing scenario in the GLOBE LUXURIÉ CONCLAVE 2017. Besides, the conclave will be a unique platform to crystallise the business vision of the corporates from the luxury segment. It will aim to provide an impetus to the sector to maintain exclusivity in its offerings and give a boost to the UBER-LUXURY, LUXURY and AFFORDABLE LUXURY segments for luxury enthusiasts. Because there is no substitute to luxury ...

The GLOBE LUXURIE CONCLAVE 2017 is expected to lend a scaffold to the cornerstones of luxury and lay the groundwork for the corporates and audience engaged in luxury trade (tangible and experiential luxury). The eminent platform will witness contrivance of business ideas to further strengthen the luxury sector and encourage plush lifestyle.

Amidst the magnificence of the Dubai skylines and the richness of the Middle East culture, the GLOBE LUXURIÉ DÉCODÉ will unfold another golden chapter hailing luxury through the GLOBE LUXURIÉ AWARDS 2017. Before the most discerning set of audience, 101 luxury brands and personalities from different walks of life of the luxury segment will be felicitated for their outstanding contribution towards making luxury the way of life and exploring opulence to its best.

Panel Discussion

The GLOBE Platinum Awards 2016 is an Asia-Pacific initiative that acknowledges and rewards brands and leaders across the Asia-Pacific region for their business excellence in taking their companies through the path of success with innovative approach and strong work ethics. PricewaterhouseCoopers P.L. (PwC), which is a multinational integrated professional services organisation and one of the biggest international auditing firms, will be the process reviewer for the complete project.

It will not only be reviewing the brands for their innovation and achievements but will also be evaluating the comprehensive excellence of the businesses in their respective domains. The GLOBE Platinum Awards 2016 will encompass the whole gamut of businesses across the Asia-Pacific region, with special focus on the service and infrastructure industries.
